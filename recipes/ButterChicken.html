miam miam Butter Chicken

Description

Take a good look at this image, you can smell it, can't you ?

Spicy yet smooth all at once, those tender chicken pieces bathed delicately in that creamy sauce, that naan both fluffy and crispy at the same time which, after one bite, releases a rush of cheese melting in your mouth. Just close your eyes and imagine savoring this magnificent dish straight from India

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken pieces(often thighs or breasts)
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Turmeric, Garam masala, Chili powder, Cumin, coriander powder, Paprika powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • Fresh cream
  • Sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
    2.
    • Pat the chicken pieces dry.
    • In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and a mix of spices: turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, cumin, salt.
    • Add the chicken pieces, coat them well, cover and refrigerate. Minimum 30 minutes; better if a few hours or overnight.
  2. Cook the chicken
    3.
    • Heat oil (and a little butter if you like) in a pan over medium-high heat.
    • Add the marinated chicken and cook until the pieces are lightly browned and partially cooked through. They will finish cooking in the sauce.
    • Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Prepare the sauce
    4.
    • In the same pan, melt butter (and/or oil) and add the chopped onion. Saut√© until translucent or light golden.
    • Add ginger-garlic paste, stir for a minute until fragrant.
    • Add spices: cumin, garam masala, coriander powder, chili/paprika etc. Toast them briefly to release aroma.
    • Add the tomato puree or chopped tomatoes, sugar (if using), salt, and let the mixture simmer until the sauce starts to thicken and the raw tomato flavour softens.
    • Add the cream and kasuri methi (fenugreek leaves) toward the end, stir to combine. This gives that rich creamy and slightly aromatic finish.
  4. Combine chicken and sauce
    5.
    • Return the partially-cooked chicken pieces into the sauce. Mix thoroughly so the sauce coats the chicken.
    • Let everything simmer together on low heat for about 5-10 minutes (or more) so the chicken finishes cooking and absorbs the sauce flavour.
  5. Finish and serve
    6.
    • Serve with warm cheese naan.
